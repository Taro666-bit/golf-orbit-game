Golf Orbit

Experience the thrill of golf in space with Golf Orbit, a unique physics-based golf game that challenges your precision and strategy.

How to Play Golf Orbit

Golf Orbit combines the precision of golf with the unpredictable physics of space. Your goal is to navigate through challenging courses by hitting the ball into the hole with as few strokes as possible.

  1. Aim your shot by dragging and adjusting the direction arrow
  2. Control power by how far you pull back
  3. Release to shoot and watch your ball navigate through space
  4. Complete each hole with the fewest strokes possible
  5. Progress through increasingly challenging levels

Game Features

Unique Space Physics

Experience gravity fields, wormholes, and other space phenomena that affect your golf ball's trajectory.

Multiple Planets

Play on various planets and celestial bodies, each with their own unique gravity and challenges.

Skill Progression

Start with simple courses and progress to increasingly complex and challenging levels.

Beautiful Graphics

Enjoy stunning visuals of space environments with colorful planets, stars, and cosmic phenomena.

Tips & Tricks

  • Pay attention to the gravitational pull of nearby planets
  • Use minimal power for precise short-distance shots
  • Take advantage of gravity fields to curve your shots around obstacles
  • Study the course before taking your shot to plan the best trajectory
  • Sometimes a bank shot off walls or obstacles is the best approach
  • Remember that different planets have different gravity strengths
